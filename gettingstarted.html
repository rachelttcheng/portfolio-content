Getting Started

The age old gym question: how do you motivate yourself to go? *insert stuff here about that*

Never underestimate the importance of wearing something you feel cute in to the gym! Get some nice flat-soled shoes too so you can feel the ground during leg day. I will explain later.

What do you do after a workout? Make sure you get your protein in.

Set goals for yourself, and plan out what you're going to do at the gym before you go so you don't get anxious when you're there
